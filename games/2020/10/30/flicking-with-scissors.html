I developed Flicking with Scissors as a way to play with Unity. The code is written in C# and runs on Android devices. Unity really cut down on the development time and allowed me to put the game together pretty quickly.

The goal is to defeat as many enemies as possible using the rules of Rock, Paper, Scissors. Slide (or flick) the character on the bottom to defeat one of the enemy characters on the top as quickly as possible. As you progress you have less time to defeat each enemy. There is always at least one enemy that can defeated.

  • The game is available on the Google Play Store.
  • The source code is available on Bitbucket
